About MIT-MOL Conveyors Pvt. Ltd.

Leading the way in conveyor solutions since 1990

Our Story

MIT-MOL is one of the leading designers and manufacturers of unit, powder, and bulk material handling conveyors and equipment since 1990. Our products are found in practically every industry and are relied upon to improvise efficiency, maximize production, and reduce labor costs.

We are committed to providing high-quality solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients. With our extensive experience and cutting-edge technology, we have become a trusted partner for many organizations, ensuring that their material handling processes are optimized for performance and reliability.

Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to design, manufacture, and install custom-built conveyor systems tailored to your specific requirements. Whether you need simple belt conveyors or complex automation systems, MIT-MOL has the expertise to deliver robust and effective solutions.
