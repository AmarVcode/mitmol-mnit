Chain Conveyor Systems

Reliable Solutions for Moving Materials Through Production Lines

Why Choose Our Chain Conveyor Systems?

Highly reliable and durable for heavy-duty applications.

Efficiently transport heavy unit loads with ease.

Easy installation and minimal maintenance required.

Ideal for various industries, including automotive, steel, and food processing.

Our Chain Conveyor Systems

Apron Chain Conveyors

Designed for moving loose bulk materials like coal, lime, sand, and stone. Commonly used in the mining industry.

Pusher Chain Conveyors

Ideal for conveying rigid packages or unit loads with an even base by pushing them over a fixed bedplate. Used in steel and dairy industries.

Slat Chain Conveyors

Consist of chains with attached non-interlocking slats or metal flights. Widely used in appliance assembly lines and unit handling applications.

Pallet Chain Conveyors

Engineered for moving large, heavy loads such as pallets and drums. Suitable for chill or cold stores with special low-temperature components.

Rod Chain Conveyors

Commonly used in the bakery sector, featuring link chains and skewers. Made from chromium or cold-drawn carbon steel for durability.

Carrier Chain Conveyors

Designed with attachments for conveying parts and assemblies, widely used in the steel industry.

Ensuring Maximum Uptime

Our chain conveyor systems are built for durability and efficiency, ensuring maximum uptime in your operations. We follow best practices for maintaining peak performance, including:

  • Regular inspections to ensure optimal performance and identify potential issues before they arise.
  • Close monitoring of motors, reducers, and other critical components to avoid unexpected failures.
  • Maintaining an inventory of key parts to minimize downtime during repairs or maintenance.
  • Comprehensive training for your personnel to ensure efficient and safe operation of the conveyor systems.
  • Proactive maintenance strategies to extend the lifespan of your equipment and reduce long-term costs.
  • Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools to predict and prevent potential failures.
